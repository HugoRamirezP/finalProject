Training

Proper training for all construction equipment is essential. It important for the operator to learn how to operate in a safely and proper manner. There is a lot to learn when it comes to such heavy construction equipment. Preventive maintance checks and services is something an operator should do daily. It is a good habit they teach in training to inspect your equipment before operation.

Training Facilities

  • CICA Academy
  • International Union of Operating Engineers
  • OE3 JATC

Beginners Training

First thing during training is rules and regulations. During this time you will learn the do's and don't. You will go over safety training like Cal/OSHA to provide awareness of common job-related safety and health hazards. you will also be informed of Personal Protective Equipment(PPE) that will be clothing, helmet, glasses, and any other equipment that can help prevent and protect the wearer from injury or chemical exposure.

Hands On

During training the best way to learn is on the seat getting familiar with the controls of the equipment. Through out your training you will learn in class nomeclatures about the equipment along with other important book studies. Then you will be going out to the training field. spending a few days to a week getting hands on training with different machines.


You will do a proper inspection of the equipment prior to use. That will get you in a good habit of inspecting it everyday along with familiarizing yourself with the location of the master power, coolant, oil stick, hydraulic fluid, fan belt, and other parts of the machines essential for proper operation.
